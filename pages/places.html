Monuments

PlaceDistrictNoteTip
Basilica Cistern [map]SultanahmetUnderground Roman reservoir, 336 columns, Medusa heads. Eerie.Cold and dim; bring a layer
Blue Mosque (Sultanahmet Camii) [d] [map]Sultanahmet20,000+ blue İznik tiles, five minarets. Free entry.Go early morning or late afternoon to avoid crowds
Dolmabahçe Palace [d] [map]BeşiktaşTurkey's largest palace, world's largest ballroom. On the Bosphorus waterfront.~€6; 2–3 hours. Free to walk the gardens and gate
Eyüp Sultan Mosque [map]EyüpSacred Muslim pilgrimage site. Ottoman mosque complex at the Golden Horn.Respectful dress required; pilgrimage atmosphere different from tourist mosques
Galata Tower [d] [map]Karaköy360° views from a 14th-century Genoese watchtower.€40 entry; book ahead or queue early
Hagia Sophia [***] [map]SultanahmetByzantine masterpiece, now a mosque. 537 CE. Essential.€25 upper gallery; buy online
Haydarpaşa Station [map]KadıköyGrand 1908 German-built railway terminus. Closed but beautiful from outside.No entry; view from the ferry or walk the waterfront
Hippodrome [map]SultanahmetAncient chariot racing arena. Egyptian Obelisk (1600 BCE) still standing.Free, open air, walk through
Kamondo Steps [map]KaraköyCurved Art Nouveau staircase. Heavily photographed.Free; best early morning without crowds
Maiden's Tower (Kız Kulesi) [map]ÜsküdarSmall tower on an islet offshore. More atmospheric from a distance.Best photographed from Üsküdar waterfront
Ortaköy Mosque [d] [map]OrtaköyWaterside mosque, gorgeous interior, free entry. Plus kumpir stands nearby.Best with the Bosphorus Bridge view; go in the late afternoon
Rumeli Hisarı [map]BosphorusOttoman fortress built in 4 months to blockade Constantinople (1452).Walk the walls; great Bosphorus views
Rüstem Paşa Camii [map]EminönüSmall mosque with exceptional İznik tiles. Usually uncrowded.Above a market arcade — easy to walk past; look for the staircase
Sirkeci Station [map]SirkeciHistoric 1890 railway terminal. Terminus of the Orient Express.Now serves Marmaray commuter rail; visit the historic waiting room
Sokollu Mehmet Paşa Camii [map]SultanahmetQuiet Sinan mosque off the tourist drag. Exquisite tilework.Usually very uncrowded
Süleymaniye Mosque [map]EminönüSinan's masterpiece, dominates the third hill. Less crowded than Blue Mosque.Walk up from the Grand Bazaar; great city views from the courtyard
Topkapı Palace [**] [map]SultanahmetOttoman imperial palace for 400 years. Treasury, Harem, pavilions.Harem = separate ticket; closed Tuesday; buy online at 9am
Yedikule Museum [map]YedikuleFortress at the southern end of the land walls. Climb the towers.Small entry fee; combine with nearby Safa meyhane
Yeni Camii [map]Eminönü17th-century mosque on the waterfront. Pigeons everywhere.Free; right at the Eminönü ferry docks
Şemsi Paşa Camii [map]ÜsküdarSinan-designed (1580), beautifully on the Üsküdar waterfront.Tiny and usually quiet; right by the ferry dock

Pera Museum [map]BeyoğluGood rotating exhibitions, Orientalist paintings collection.Check current exhibition online before visiting

Markets
