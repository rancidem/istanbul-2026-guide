Transport 2026

The Istanbulkart — Essential

Your key to the city. Get one immediately at the airport. The card works on metro, tram, ferries, buses, and funiculars.

Card purchase:165 TL (~€3.30)
Single ride:42 TL (metro/tram/bus)
Ferry:53-59 TL
Transfer discount:~30% off within 2 hours
Savings:~50% cheaper than single tickets

Where to buy: Airport machines (English available), metro stations, kiosks
Strategy: Load 3-4 days worth at once (~500-800 TL), not daily
Tip: Top up before leaving each area — not every station has machines

[!] February 2026 Update: Transport fares increased 20% on Feb 16, 2026. Previous increases: 35% (Jan 2025) + 30% (Sept 2025). Budget accordingly.

Getting Around

Metro (M-Lines)

  • M1: Airport (IST) → City center. Runs 24h.
  • M2: Levent ↔ Yenikapı via Taksim. Connects Beyoğlu to southern lines.
  • M4: Kadıköy ↔ Sabiha Gökçen Airport (SAW). Essential for Asian side.
  • M11: NEW airport line — fastest way to/from IST.

Tram T1 — Your Tourist Lifeline

Route: Kabataş → Karaköy → Eminönü → Sultanahmet → Grand Bazaar → Beyazıt

This is the main tourist tram. Can be very crowded in summer. Runs 6am-11pm.

Ferry (Vapur) — The Best €1 You'll Spend

The 20-minute crossing between Europe and Asia isn't just transport — it's an experience.

RouteDurationPriceExperience
Eminönü ↔ Kadıköy20 min~53 TLClassic crossing
Karaköy ↔ Kadıköy15 min~53 TLQuick hop
Eminönü ↔ Üsküdar10 min~53 TLShortest crossing
Eminönü → Anadolu Kavağı90 min~80 TLBosphorus tour

Ferry philosophy: Sit outside on the back deck, order tea (çay) from the onboard vendor, feed simit to seagulls. Morning ferries (before 9am) are peaceful; sunset ferries are magical.

Funicular

  • F1: Kabataş → Taksim (1 stop, avoids steep hill)
  • F2 (Tünel): Karaköy → Beyoğlu (world's second-oldest metro, 1875)

Taxi / Ride Share

  • BiTaksi or iTaksi apps — metered, safer than hailing
  • Meter opening: 65 TL + 44 TL/km
  • Short trip minimum: 210 TL
  • Tip: Avoid taxis at Sultanahmet offering "fixed prices" — almost always a scam

Key Routes for Tourists

Sultanahmet to Taksim

Tram T1 to Kabataş → Funicular F1 to Taksim (15 min total)

European Side to Asian Side

Ferry from Eminönü or Karaköy to Kadıköy (20 min, ~53 TL)

Airport to Sultanahmet

Metro M11 to Gayrettepe → M2 to Vezneciler → Walk or tram to Sultanahmet (50-70 min)

Useful Apps

  • BiTaksi — Licensed taxi booking
  • Trafi — Public transport navigation (best for Istanbul)
  • Moovit — Transit routing alternative
  • Google Maps — Walking directions (less reliable for transit timing)
>> Remember: The ferry crossing is part of your Istanbul experience, not just a way to get from A to B. Take the slow ferry. Order tea. Watch the seagulls follow the boat. This is the city showing you its soul.
