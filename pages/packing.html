Carry-on (Keep-with-you essentials)

Documents & Money

  • Passport + paper copy + phone scan (offline)
  • Printed flight + hotel confirmations
  • Travel insurance + emergency contacts
  • Wallet: 2 cards (separate 1) + EUR cash
  • Backup cash/card stash
  • Prescription copy (if needed)
  • Pen

Health (Carry-on only)

  • Prescription meds + 2 extra days
  • Mini kit: pain relief, antihistamine, plasters + blister pads
  • Hand sanitizer + tissues

Electronics (Carry-on only)

  • Phone + charger + wall plug
  • Spare charging cable
  • Power bank
  • Headphones
  • Universal adapter (Type C/F)
  • eSIM/SIM plan info
  • Optional: luggage tracker

Delay-proof Spare Outfit

  • 1 top
  • 1 underwear
  • 1 pair socks

Flight Comfort

  • Empty water bottle
  • 1–2 snack bars
  • Lip balm
  • Earplugs + eye mask
  • Compression socks (optional)

Airport Security (EU)

  • Liquids ≤100ml in clear 1L bag
  • Batteries/power bank: carry-on only
  • Liquids + electronics easy to reach

Checked Bag (Wardrobe + bulk liquids)

Outer Layers

  • 1 waterproof shell (hood ideal)
  • 1 warm mid-layer (puffer or fleece)

Tops

  • 3 long-sleeve tops
  • 2 short-sleeve tops
  • 1 nicer top (dinner/rooftop)

Bottoms

  • 2 pants (jeans/chinos/technical)
  • 1 comfy bottom (joggers/leggings)

Thermals (for cold days)

  • 1 thermal top (thin)
  • 1 thermal bottom (optional)

Underwear & Socks

  • 7–8 underwear
  • 6–7 socks (1–2 warm pairs)

Sleep

  • 1 sleep set

Warm Accessories (High Impact)

  • 1 scarf/shawl (warmth + mosque)
  • 1 beanie
  • 1 pair light gloves

Shoes

  • 1 primary walking shoe (broken-in) — wear on travel day
  • 1 backup pair (water-resistant)

Toiletries (Liquids Here)

  • Toothbrush + toothpaste
  • Deodorant
  • Shampoo/conditioner/body wash
  • Moisturizer
  • Sunscreen (small)
  • Razor/grooming kit
  • Contact lens supplies (if needed)

Day-to-day Extras

  • Small daypack or crossbody (zippered)
  • Sunglasses
  • Compact umbrella (optional)
  • Wet wipes (optional)
  • Laundry bag / packing cubes (optional)

Checked-bag Safety

  • Luggage tag outside + contact card inside
  • Photo of checked bag (for lost luggage)
  • Do NOT pack passport, meds, power bank, valuables

Wear-on-the-plane (Saves space/weight)

  • Bulkiest shoes
  • Mid-layer (puffer/fleece)
  • Waterproof shell
  • Scarf (plane blanket)
[*] Pro Tip: Layering is key for Istanbul's variable February weather. Pack items that work together in combinations. [Print]
